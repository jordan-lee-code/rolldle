# Image credits

Every photograph in Rolldle is openly licensed.

Most rounds use the shared image pool in `public/images/pool/`, gathered from many
Wikimedia Commons contributors by `scripts/fetch-pool.mjs`. The bulk of those are CC0
or public domain and need no attribution; for any that do, the photographer, source,
and licence are recorded in `public/images/pool/manifest.json`. That manifest is the
full, honest record, kept with the code so the credit exists even though the game
doesn't show it for every image.

A few breads are shown with specific, verified photos, and those photographers are
named in the app's About panel as well as here:

- **Barm cake with black pudding**, by Wendy Mann, CC BY 2.0
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Barm_cake_with_black_pudding.jpg
- **Stotty cake**, by Mariegriffiths, CC BY-SA 3.0
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Stotty1.JPG
- **Belfast bap**, by 159753, CC0 1.0
  https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Belfast_Bap.jpg

If you grow the pool or pin a new photo, keep `manifest.json` and `credits.js` honest,
and check each licence on its Commons page.
